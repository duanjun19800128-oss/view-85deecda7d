Data Boundary · Why dealers will actually adopt this 数据边界 · Dealer 真的愿意用 Portal 的前提

If we ask dealers for retail prices and end-customer info, no one signs up. This boundary is contractual. 如果系统要 dealer 报终端售价和客户信息，没人愿意上。这条边界写在合同里。

✓ FOA Side CAN See
  • 📦 Dealer order: SKU, qty, amount, shipping warehouse
  • 📋 Quote → conversion rate per dealer
  • 🏠 Project type, budget band (range), prep completeness
  • 🚚 Fulfillment status (shipped / in transit / delivered)
  • 🛠 Support ticket and resolution time
  • 📊 Inventory levels in all 6 warehouses
  • 📈 Loss reasons (price / competitor / etc.)
✗ FOA Side CANNOT See
  • 💰 Dealer's selling price to end customer
  • 📊 Dealer's gross margin or markup %
  • 👤 End customer name, contact, address
  • 👥 Dealer's internal employee salaries
  • 🏢 Dealer's overhead or operating cost
  • 🔄 Dealer's other brands' inventory data
  • 📁 Dealer's customer file / CRM
Why this matters strategically

Most cabinet/furniture wholesale "dealer portals" fail because the wholesaler tries to get the end-customer data too. Dealers refuse — they protect their customers like their life. FOA Cabinet OS is positioned as a B2B supplier service layer, not a Dealer surveillance system. This makes adoption fundamentally different.
